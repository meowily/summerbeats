Get Involved

Want to be Part of the Festival?

Are you outgoing, like to put smiles on people’s faces, feel the joy of being part of something big then consider joining our team! We are always looking for like-minded people. You will work with a diverse group of energetic people who are joined together through their love of all types. If you’re interested, have a look through the opportunities available before, during and after the festival.

We have many jobs and volunteer work assignments with many benefits. We include 3 meals a day, staff camping, and you will have access to all the stages when you are not working. Please be aware that we may receive more applications than spots available. Please do not feel disappointed and please do reapply next year. Many thanks from all of us.

Security

Breaking fights, offer assistance, provide venue information, scan crowd behavior, present at ticket counters & parking lots, be the first to respond to an emergency in a crowd.

Job opportunity email resume to summerbeatsjobs@gmail.com

Food

There are three outdoor cafeterias to work at. There will also be food trucks but they are not connected to the Summer Beats Festival to learn more about the job availability for the food truck visit Food Truck Schedule.

Job opportunity email resume to summerbeatsjobs@gmail.com

Set-Up/ Take Down

Setting up and taking down tents, lights, maps, signs, ect. Also at the end of the festival you will be required to help the clean-up crew for 2 hours. This is a volunteer job to apply please fill out the PDF.

Job opportunity email resume to summerbeatsjobs@gmail.com
