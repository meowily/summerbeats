Night Bass takes the party to Peridot Stage

Night Bass is an American record and party label company founded in 2014 by AC Slater. He was looking for an alternative to the Los Angeles club scene when he came up with the idea to create a record label company featuring music he loved. To make this happen he needed help. He shared his ideas with Dim Mak (link to dim mak website) and together they changed the party landscape by showcasing groundbreaking artists. AC Slater says “I want the term Night Bass to be a description of the music. Not a genre but more like a wider sound or feeling”.

Rottun Records Shakes on Black Opal Stage

Rottun Records is a Canadian record label founded in 2005 by Jeff Abel, better known by his stage name Excision. He is known for focusing on atmosphere and minimalistic percussion. He works with various artists like Datsik, Downlink, Ajapai, Liquid Stranger and more. Excision is from Kelowna, British Columbia. He was one of the first dubstep producers and Djs in North America having his first release in 2007.

Future Classic unplugs on Selenite Stage

Future Classic is an Australian record and party label company founded in 2004 by Nathan McLay. This Australian underground label became known around the world in less then 2 years. They are one of the biggest players on the festival circuits. Lots of known DJ’s with this company including Flume, Ta-ku, Cashmere Cat, Chrome Sparks and more.
