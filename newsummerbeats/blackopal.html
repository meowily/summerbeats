Black Opal

Sponcered by Rottun Records

Black Opal is known as the “eye stone” or fire energy”. Like lightening in a rainbow. Flashes its brilliance everyone notices its fire. Acts as a prism within the aura. Considered to be lucky. Gaze into the past, present or future. Power stone for magic and can be used for “reading eyes”.

Heavy dubstep music with harsh electro house and heavy metal influences. Mixture of house, garage, bassline and grime with a dash of old skool rave. Feeling of freedom!

PK Sound makes sure you will hear it all – brilliantly, precisely and consistently. Powerful PK Sounds will be on stage.
