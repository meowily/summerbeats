Tickets

camp & ticket packages

All campsites include an area for your tent, bathroom amenities, and power outlets. No Dogs allowed on site. Families welcome. All Campers must have wrist bands. Each site comes with 2 wrist bands. Additional wrist bands can be purchased.

Camp Cyan $150

  • Outdoor no heated public showers
  • Public toilets
  • 2 hammocks per campground
  • 5 Public charging stations

Camp Pagoda $250

  • Outdoor heated public showers
  • Public toilets
  • 2 hammocks per campground
  • 8 Public charging stations

Camp Zen $350

  • Indoor heated private showers
  • Private toilets
  • 2 hammocks per campground
  • Private electric outlets
