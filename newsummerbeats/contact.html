FAQ

What do I need to bring? Sunscreen, hat, sunglasses, comfortable clothes, rain jacket, spending money and of course camping gear!

Can I bring my pets? No these rules are strictly enforced please find other arrangements for them while you are here.  

Can I bring a cooking camp stove? Yes, but there are restrictions. Must be, propane fueled, certification from nationally recognized testing organization, responsible enough to never leave stove unattended.

Food

You are welcome to bring your own food. Cooking areas will be set up next to picnic tables.
Or
Chow down at the three cafeterias home to delicious food, cool drinks, sweets and yummies. Open all day and all night.

Getting There

We’ve made traveling easy with our TravelEasy mobile app, with lots of ways getting to the festival. This app has all the information about getting to our festival. Includes information about shuttle service, taxi rates, interactive maps, and a carpool finder. We suggest everyone downloads this app onto their phone to see show times and other important information.
